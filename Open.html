Deployable Air-Brake System

Interactive visualization of a deployable high-power rocket air-brake assembly. This viewer was created to communicate the mechanical packaging, electronics integration, servo actuation, and transparent enclosure design more clearly than a static render.

Why this viewer

After learning that SOLIDWORKS Visualize is part of the workflow, I spent time learning the tool and iterating through camera angles, materials, transparency, and render settings to create a clearer presentation of the assembly.
