Purpose
I created this viewer after learning that SOLIDWORKS Visualize is used in industry workflows. The goal was to better communicate mechanical packaging, electronics integration, transparency, and servo actuation beyond a static render.
Focus Areas
  • Electronics and servo integration
  • Transparent enclosure visibility
  • Mechanical packaging and mounting
  • Render iteration and design communication

Deployable Air-Brake System

Interactive

SOLIDWORKS Visualize Multi Orbit 360 Viewer

Drag inside the frame to rotate around the assembly. This interactive viewer shows the air-brake system from multiple angles so the electronics, servo actuator, transparent enclosure, and mechanical structure can be inspected more clearly.

What this shows

  • Progression from CAD model to higher-fidelity presentation render.
  • Transparent material tuning to reveal the internal mechanism.
  • Camera and composition choices that make the design easier to understand.

If the viewer does not load, make sure the Visualize-generated Data folder is uploaded next to this page.
